LOS ANGELES

in L.a everyone is a star

in a nutshell

The mistake many people make when they come to LA is expecting it to be like any other city - except maybe bigger. In fact, LA is like no other city. It’s a region unto itself: a sprawling network of suburbs, beaches, bars, clubs, restaurants and cafés – home to some 30 million people. Once you embrace that fact, things somehow becomes simpler. You stop worrying about the long drives. You embrace the theme park atmosphere of Venice Beach. You realise this a place built for good living, rather than tourists – and that that makes it one of the most captivating destinations there is.

GETTING AROUND

Public transportation is a no go. Walking – meh. Your best bet is to rent a car. It´s L.A what do you want?

KNOWN FOR

Santa Monica, Venice, Arnold, Hollywood, Broken Dreams, The Dude.

WHEN TO GO

Anytime.

PARIS

paris is always a good idea

in a nutshell

A city that conforms to your expectations – in the best way possible. Paris is diminutive patisseries selling exquisite little cakes. Paris is the quirky brilliance of the Pompidou Centre, and the grandeur of the Louvre. The city’s famous conservatism mean the central arrondissements are as romantic as they were in the grand old days of the Second Empire. It’s easy to laugh at the Paris clichés all around you – at the accordion players and bistros on every street corner. But it’s much better just to relax and enjoy them.

GETTING AROUND

Explore Paris by renting a bicycle or just walking. The metro is very extensive.

KNOWN FOR

Culinary center, open-air markets, romantic walks, The Eifel Tower, The Louvre and much much more.

WHAT TO EAT

Pain Au Chocolat, Parisian Bistro food, Cheese, more Cheese, Wine.

HOW MUCH

Dinner for 2 – 50€
Beer – 5€-7€

WHEN TO GO

May to September

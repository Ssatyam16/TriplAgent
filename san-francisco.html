SAN FRANCISCO

has only one drawback – it’s hard to leave

in a nutshell

Pioneer spirit dies hard in Frisco. 150 years after the Gold Rush, it remains a place for mavericks and nonconformists. Ageing hippies still walk the streets, dreaming of a more peaceable Land of the Free; tomorrow’s tech entrepreneurs huddle over lattés and Macbooks in coffee shops so hip they don’t have names yet. Here, you can hike up snow-capped mountains, join jazz musicians for an on-stage jam, or wash away your troubles in the Pacific ocean. San Francisco is a city where anything seems possible – perfect for those in search of liberation.

GETTING AROUND

Walking in SF is your best bet. It´s a small city that you can easily cover on foot or by bike.

KNOWN FOR

Golden Gate Bridge, Silicon Valley, Hipsters, Alcatraz, GLBT community.

WHAT TO EAT

Asian (japanese, chinese, korean, thai, etc.) vegetarian/ vegan-friendly, seafood.

HOW MUCH

Dinner for 2 – $70
Beer – $5-$8

WHEN TO GO

September to November.
